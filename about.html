    • Incorporated in the year 2004, Aase Texwell is a leading enterprise engaged in the manufacturing and supplying of a variety of Cotton Fabrics. Under the profound headship of Proprietors Mr. RK Muthusamy, we have marked a distinct position for ourselves in the industry.

    Warehouse

    Our spacious warehouse outfitted with all essential amenities has assisted us to earn a good reputation in the marketplace. Besides, we have an independent documentation shipping agency tie-up which moves in perfect co-ordination with the buyers as well as suppliers.

    Team Power

    Our highly qualified and expert team has assisted us to successfully meet the needs and demands of our patrons. They work in absolute coordination to render best possible services to customers that too within stipulated time frame.

    Quality Assurance

    Quality is our prime concern thus we follow strict quality procedures to keep a tab on the quality of products manufactured by us. Our quality experts have a sound knowledge of the quality standards that need to be adhered. They conduct random tests at the each stage of manufacturing process.

    Packaging

    Packaging is very essential part and we make sure each and every product manufactured by us, is safely packed and stored. For this, we make use of quality Yarn bags for the packaging of products. Moreover, we put all the necessary details of the products on the packaging to ensure easy recognition.

    Clientele

    With our customer oriented approach we have been able to attract a large number of revered patrons. Our valuable clients pertain to every nook and corner of the globe such as Europe, South Korea, Turkey, USA etc.
